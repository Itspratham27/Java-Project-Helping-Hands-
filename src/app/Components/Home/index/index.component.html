Daily Feeding Program

We serve daily meals to people in need across India.

Whether it is freshly cooked food or raw grains, we’re all hands on deck to take care of the food requirements for your beneficiaries.

Helping Hands is a not for profit organization, designing interventions to reduce hunger among underserved communities in India.

In January 2022, Helping Hands (registered as Hunger Heroes) collaborated to eradicate hunger and malnutrition in India.

All efforts are concerted towards providing better food to more people. Through our targeted interventions for child and maternal malnutrition, we are working towards bringing large-scale systemic transformation in the nutrition landscape of India.

Daily Feeding Program

Despite the significant progress India has made, a large number of people still face the risk of food insecurity. The COVID-19 pandemic has further exacerbated the challenge of hunger in our country.

As a response to the devastating consequences of the pandemic, Helping Hands Organization launched the
Helping Hands Program in January 2022
to provide daily meal support to the most vulnerable families.

Under this program, Helping Hands has served 4.3+ crore meals with the main focus on reducing hunger for the most vulnerable.
